# Wispear — Branding Brief

**Project: Wispear (wispear.ai)**

**What it is:**
Wispear is a recommendation engine for AI agent components (skills, LLMs, MCP servers, APIs). Users describe their need, Wispear recommends the right tools to use, validated by a community of curators on an on-chain knowledge graph (Intuition Protocol).

**Name origin:**
- **Wispear** = portmanteau of "whisper" + "pear"
- Core concept: a pear that whispers to your AI agent what tools to use
- Hidden bonus: "ear" is embedded in the name — "wisp-ear"
- Hidden bonus: "peer" resonates too — peer-to-peer curation
- Pronunciation is very close to "whisper" in English

**Domain:** wispear.ai

**Slogan:** "Collective wisdom, whispered to your agent."

**Branding direction:**
- Logo: minimalist pear silhouette with subtle sound waves / whisper lines emanating from it
- Style: clean, modern, tech-forward but playful. Not corporate, not cute. Smart and approachable.
- Flat design, no 3D, no skeuomorphism
- Palette: fresh greens, soft gradients, with a tech accent (deep purple or electric blue)
- The pear should feel intelligent, not fruity — think "AI companion" not "grocery store"
- Variations needed: icon only (pear mark), wordmark only (wispear), combined mark, dark/light mode versions

**Tone:** Confident, minimal, slightly playful. Like a senior dev who also has good taste.
